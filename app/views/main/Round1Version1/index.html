What you need to know

Changes to the early years foundation stage framework will become statutory in September 2021.

The changes to the early years foundation stage framework apply in England. Different rules apply in Wales and Scotland.

You can read the early years foundation stage framework (PDF, 479KB, 43 pages).

The Development Matters non-statutory guidance (PDF, 975KB, 77 pages) can also help you to meet the requirements of the early years foundation stage framework.
