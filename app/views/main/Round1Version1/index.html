Help for early years providers

Changes to the early years foundation stage (EYFS) framework become law in September 2021. Access resources, advice and support to help you apply the changes.

Published 22 January 2020
From: Department for Education

Applies to: England

Use this service to:

  • find out about changes to the early years foundation stage (EYFS) framework
  • understand the requirements for each area of learning and development
  • get ideas for activities you can do with the children
  • find advice and support

This service is for:

  • childminders
  • nursery leaders
  • nursery and pre-school practitioners
